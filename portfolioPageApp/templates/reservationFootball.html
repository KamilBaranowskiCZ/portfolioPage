It is a Django app that allows you to meet other people for football matches. The application is available to residents of two cities with the possibility of further extend. Registered users can add football pitches and create matches. Each pitch must have its location, which is then displayed on the map on city page. Logged in users can sign up for matches and comment on them. The app is available in Polish language.
